# HR-Analytics-Dashoard-
## 1. Project Title / Headline

HR Analytics Dashboard | Employee Attrition & Workforce Insights using Power BI

## 2. Short Description / Purpose

This Power BI dashboard provides a comprehensive analysis of workforce data, helping organizations understand employee attrition, workforce demographics, job satisfaction, and employee performance. The dashboard enables HR teams and management to identify retention challenges, monitor workforce trends, and make data-driven human resource decisions.

## 4. Data Source

The dashboard is built using HR employee data containing:

Employee Information
Department Details
Job Roles
Attrition Status
Employee Age
Gender
Education Level
Salary Information
Job Satisfaction Ratings
Years at Company
Work Experience

The dataset was cleaned, transformed, and modeled using Power Query and Power BI to create meaningful HR insights.

## 5. Features / Highlights
📊 Business Problem

Employee attrition can significantly impact organizational productivity and recruitment costs. HR departments need a centralized solution to monitor employee trends, identify risk factors, and improve retention strategies.

🎯 Dashboard Objectives
Analyze employee attrition trends.
Monitor workforce demographics.
Evaluate department-wise employee distribution.
Identify factors influencing employee turnover.
Track job satisfaction and employee engagement.
Support HR decision-making through actionable insights.
📈 Key Performance Indicators (KPIs)
Total Employees
Attrition Count
Attrition Rate (%)
Active Employees
Average Employee Age
Average Salary
Average Years at Company
🔍 Dashboard Features
Employee Attrition Analysis
Department-wise Workforce Distribution
Job Role Performance Insights
Gender Diversity Analysis
Age Group Segmentation
Education Field Analysis
Salary Band Distribution
Job Satisfaction Tracking
Interactive Filters and Slicers
Dynamic KPI Cards and Visualizations
💡 Key Insights Generated
Identifies departments with the highest attrition rates.
Highlights employee age groups most likely to leave.
Analyzes attrition trends across job roles and education levels.
Evaluates employee satisfaction and retention patterns.
Provides workforce diversity and demographic insights.
🚀 Business Impact
Supports proactive employee retention strategies.
Helps HR teams identify workforce challenges early.
Reduces turnover-related costs through data-driven decisions.
Improves workforce planning and talent management.
Enhances organizational performance through HR analytics.
